The New Zealand Labour Party Wins Landslide In Parliament

by @bepis, October 17th, 2020 at 1:23 PM

The centre-left New Zealand Labour Party, under the leadership of Jacinda Ardern, cruised to a landslide victory in last year's general election, winning an outright majority in the House of Representatives (a very rare occurance!) and 49% of the vote. The centre-right National Party took second, with only 27% of the vote. For reference, the last time someone won an outright majority in the NZ parliament was in 1990.

Trump Has Recently Filed An Emergency Request To The Supreme Court, Asking Them To Block The Releasing Of His Tax Returns To A Grand Jury.

by @SpookyMoosh, October 17th, 2020 at 1:13 PM

U.S. President Donald Trump has recently filed an emergency request to the Supreme Court, asking them to block the releasing of his tax returns. This is coming after a federal appeals court made the decision that the Manhattan D.A., Cyrus Vance, can subpeona for the release of the president's tax returns and business record. President Trump's attorneys have been trying to get the Supreme Court to overturn the decision. Mr. Trump has been known for trying to stop his tax returns from reaching a grand jury, so this decision has been a large blow to the president.
